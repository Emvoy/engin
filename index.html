WELCOME

CT&T Associated Engineers and Consultants Limited is a Nigerian construction firm founded by a group of engineers with extensive experience in the built-environment industry. They take pleasure in delivering high-quality projects with high technical values and approaches. Innovation in design, planning, construction approach, and project execution is key to their service, which has earned them respect and built working relationships with major public development agencies as well as several private clients.

Vision Statement

CT&T Associated Engineers and Consultants Limited aspires to be the most prominent construction company in Nigeria. Our goal is to be the client's ideal by achieving excellence in value-added projects that are affordable, high-quality, and delivered on time. We seek to achieve a sustainable built environment by combining ideas and methodologies from our energetic, young, and analytical workforce, as well as input from stakeholders.

Mision Statement

To be a key player in the construction of a modern Nigeria.
